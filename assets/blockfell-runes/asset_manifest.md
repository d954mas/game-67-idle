# Blockfell Runes Authored Asset Kit

These meshes are project-original authored geometry for the first playable
slice. They are not third-party downloads and do not copy protected characters,
names, or models.

Runtime source:

- `src/blockfell_authored_assets.h`

Included first-pass meshes:

- hero faceted cuirass
- hero cape panel
- hero helmet crest
- enemy mask
- enemy horns
- rune spire
- rune glyph face
- gate keystone
- chest lock plate
- camp standard

Current limitation: these are small authored mesh overlays on top of the
existing shape-rendered gameplay silhouettes. They reduce the placeholder debt
but do not yet replace all procedural art.
