## cozy_ui.ttf

- Identity: **Roboto Regular** (Google Roboto family).
- License: **Apache License 2.0** — Copyright 2015 Google Inc. Redistribution and
  embedding permitted; no attribution required in-product.
- Source: restored from this repo's own history (git `574e993:assets/fonts/voxelheim_hud.ttf`,
