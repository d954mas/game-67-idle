# Game Loop Rule

Apply when a task claims a playable slice, prototype readiness, first loop,
progression, economy, reward, or live gameplay improvement.

## Questions

- What does the player do?
- What changes in response?
- What reward or progress is visible?
- Why would the player repeat the action?
- What is the next reason to continue?

## Evidence

Use a playable run, scenario, screenshot/video, task log, or product gate record.
Do not close loop work from a build-only check.
