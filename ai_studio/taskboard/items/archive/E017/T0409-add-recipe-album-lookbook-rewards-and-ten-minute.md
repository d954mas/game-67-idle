## What

Close the replay loop with a visual recipe matrix, best-look storage, cosmetic
rewards and an honest eight-round 8-12 minute first-session path.

## Done when

- [ ] Album shows six unknown silhouettes or player-authored discovered cards.
- [ ] Each recipe stores a best look and supports at least two visually different remixes.
- [ ] Cosmetic rewards occur after discoveries 1, 3 and 6 without currency or ads.
- [ ] Discovered focus items retain Essence marks; tapping an unknown pair highlights an owned main/accent route without revealing the exact awakening.
- [ ] Repeated known pairs save a remix and return the player toward a reachable unknown cell.
- [ ] Restyle returns immediately and hints at an unknown pair without a text wall.
- [ ] Five cold testers exhaust six discoveries plus two remixes in a median 8-12 minutes.
- [ ] No timer, repeated cutscene, loading or advertisement is counted as content.
