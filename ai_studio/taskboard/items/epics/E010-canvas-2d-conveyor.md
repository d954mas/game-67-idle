## Goal

Keep Canvas a small visual-asset workspace that carries sourced or generated
art through editing, provenance, technical acceptance, and reusable export.

## In scope

- Canvas operations and compact UI for visual asset work.
- Generation handoff, provenance, cleanup, style/technical gates, and export.
- Focused performance and correctness follow-ups proven by real projects.

## Out of scope

- Game runtime UI, Items/Balance Workbench, engine implementation, or owning
  external video models and heavyweight tool installations.
