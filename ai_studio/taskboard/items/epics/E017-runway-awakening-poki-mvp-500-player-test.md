## Goal

Ship a beautiful, bright, juicy `games/web-dressup` MVP to Poki and evaluate
the Runway Awakening loop with a 500-player Player Fit test.

## In scope

- Three Essences and six deterministic pure/pair awakenings.
- Thirty production wearables from the locked worn-peel pipeline.
- Eight meaningful rounds providing 8-12 minutes of first-session content.
- Responsive Dress Room, runway reveal, recipe album and Lookbook.
- Initial payload <=6,500,000 bytes, real web audio, Poki lifecycle and Game Events.
- Cold playtests, Inspector proof, release packaging and 500-player submission.

## Out of scope

- Additional Essences, multiplayer, social gallery, story chapters and dialogue.
- Currency, shop, gacha, energy, full character animation or generated video.
- Reusable engine changes; `external/neotolis-engine` remains read-only.
