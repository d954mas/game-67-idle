# Hybrid-Casual Patterns

Reusable, cross-project patterns for picking and shaping a casual/hybrid-casual
game. Source evidence: `gamedev_knowledge/sources/voodoo_new_big_three_hybrid_casual_2026-06-15.md`
(AppMagic, VOODOO's 2026 hits). Apply as direction, not as a clone target.

## Pick the concept

1. Choose a core action that is intrinsically SATISFYING in motion (physics,
   ASMR: things roll, click, tumble, melt, fill). "Satisfying sells." This is
   the same rule as [Core Loop](core_loop.md) + [Reward Feedback](reward_feedback.md):
   the core moment must read as one felt event, not a silent state change.
2. Take a PROVEN genre and add ONE small twist, rather than inventing a genre.
   (2026 example: loop/conveyor Sort Puzzle, popularized by Pixel Flow!; VOODOO's
   Marble Sort! twist = marbles are thrown so they bounce/mix.)
3. Learn-in-seconds first screen; add depth later (tighter spaces, new mechanics,
   chaotic layouts). Pairs with [Iteration Scope](iteration_scope.md) and the
   first-screen scope discipline in `AGENTS.md`.
4. Plan to scale one proven formula across themes, not one-off novelties.

## Monetization shape (when a project targets ads/IAP)

- One dominant IAP pack tends to carry most revenue (a single "main pack" was
  ~85% of revenue in one 2026 hit); design that anchor offer deliberately.
- No-Ads offer; difficulty-driven fail offers (a fair-but-tricky loop creates
  honest mistakes -> optional retries/boosts); streak bonuses; passes;
  tiered bundles; limited-time events; upgrade timers (pressure -> shortcuts).
- Rewarded ads are a SUPPORT lever (speed-ups, extra resources), not the
  load-bearer. Ad-led monetization spikes then drops retention.
- This is a product-stage concern; do not bolt monetization onto a first
  playable slice. Prove the satisfying loop first (see Definition of done).

## Retention shape

- Fast onboarding, near-constant rewards, always a visible "one more session"
  hook (daily streaks/chests, seasonal events, long-term unlocks). See
  [Meta Progression](meta_progression.md) and [FTUE](ftue.md).

## Do not

- Do not clone a specific title's content, identity, or assets; take the
  structural lesson only.
- Do not treat market estimates as exact; treat as direction.
- Do not let monetization or meta systems precede a proven, satisfying core
  moment and a readable first screen.
