+# Development iteration log

These are design and implementation iterations used to build the MVP. They do
not count toward the user's required ten final critique/fix cycles.

A final cycle may begin only after the prototype is feature-complete against
`mvp_poki_500_plan.md`, both the lead and an independent reviewer agree that it
is ready for final review, and the final-review readiness gate is recorded.
