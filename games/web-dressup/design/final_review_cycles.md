## Readiness gate

Final cycle 1 may start only when all of the following are proven:

- all six awakening results have production art and distinct silhouettes;
- the 8-12 minute path, Lookbook/remix rewards and required wearable content exist;
- native and web builds pass their automated gates;
- target responsive captures and one complete runtime recording exist;
- the lead and one independent reviewer both explicitly mark the prototype
  feature/content complete and ready for final critique.

## Cycle contract

Each of the ten final cycles requires:

1. a fresh subagent reviewer with a declared lens;
2. concrete findings against the current post-development build;
3. implementation changes addressing the accepted findings;
4. automated and/or runtime re-verification of the affected behavior;
5. a post-fix reviewer verdict.

A review without a landed fix and recheck does not count. Cycles are sequential:
cycle N+1 reviews the corrected output of cycle N.
