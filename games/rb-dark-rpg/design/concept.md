# RB Dark RPG Concept

Status: draft

## Hook

Compact dark fantasy RPG prototype: explore a ruined edge settlement, survive
short lethal encounters, and return with enough proof or loot to push one step
deeper.

## Audience

Players who like dark RPG atmosphere, readable combat decisions, and small
high-stakes runs more than idle progression or large open-world sprawl.

## Platform

Native desktop debug build first. Browser or release packaging comes after the
first playable loop is proven.

## Design Pillars

1. Readable danger: every threat should telegraph clearly enough to learn from.
2. Small RPG choices: gear, positioning, and retreat decisions matter quickly.
3. Dark but practical: mood supports play clarity, not obscured UI or controls.

## No-Go List

- No idle, prestige, offline, or away-time loop by default.
- No broad lore bible before the first playable combat/exploration slice.
- No generated final assets until source/library options are checked first.

## Open Questions

- What does `RB` stand for in the player-facing title?
- Is the first combat slice real-time action, tactical pause, or turn-based?
- Should the camera stay third-person over-the-shoulder, top-down, or fixed?
