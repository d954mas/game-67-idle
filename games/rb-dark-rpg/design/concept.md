# RB Dark RPG Concept: Дракон не вернулся

Status: draft

## Hook

`Дракон не вернулся` is a compact 2D illustrated browser RPG about an ordinary
seeker in the Ash Border, where the disappearance of the Great Dragon turns
simple paid contracts into evidence of a larger conspiracy.

## Audience

Players who like old browser RPG structure, dark but readable fantasy, quest
journals, loot, clues, and short high-stakes contract chains more than idle
progression, MMO systems, or open-world sprawl.

## Platform

2D illustrated browser/web target. Local/native debug builds may be used as the
engine validation lane, but the game fantasy and screen flow are browser-RPG
first.

## Design Pillars

1. Absence has logistics: the Dragon's disappearance is visible in closed roads,
   grain shortages, permits, contracts, and nervous officials.
2. Ordinary seeker, dangerous proof: the player is not chosen; they become
   important because a practical job exposes a clue worth killing over.
3. Quest-led browser RPG: hub, map, location, autobattle, reward, journal, and
   clue systems do the story work.
4. Dark but readable: mood supports clear choices, silhouettes, and UI, not
   realistic misery or obscured controls.

## No-Go List

- No idle, prestige, offline, or away-time loop by default.
- No free-roaming 3D/WASD first slice unless the lead explicitly redirects.
- No broad lore that is not tied to Act I quests, locations, characters, or
  clues.
- No MMO/social overload: PvP, clans, rankings, raids, or monetization
  currencies.
- No generated final assets until source/library options are checked first.
