# Voxelheim — Fake-shot prompts (theme test)

Goal: 3 gameplay fake shots that share ONE composition and differ ONLY by art
theme, so the lead chooses a **theme**, not a camera. 16:9 in-game look ("a real
game screenshot, not concept art"). Aspirational inspiration, not a pixel target.

## Shared composition (identical across all 3)

Third-person view, slightly behind and above a blocky low-poly fantasy hero
holding a sword, standing on a stone path on a snowy mountainside. Ahead, a
ruined stone keep with a glowing dungeon entrance = the tempting destination. A
dragon silhouette circles a distant peak. Readable game HUD overlay: top-left
health + stamina bars with a hero level badge, top-right round minimap, a 6-slot
hotbar centered at the bottom, and a small objective banner ("Clear the Frost
Keep") under the minimap. Clear foreground/midground/background depth.

## Theme A — Bright Roblox Adventure

`A third-person fantasy action-RPG game screenshot, blocky low-poly characters and world in the friendly Roblox style. A chunky blocky hero with a sword stands on a stone path on a snowy mountain, facing a ruined stone keep with a glowing blue dungeon entrance ahead; a small dragon silhouette circles a distant peak under a bright blue sky. Highly saturated cheerful colors, clean toy-like plastic materials, soft rounded chunky shapes, bright midday sunlight, soft shadows, very readable. Clean game UI overlay: top-left red health bar and yellow stamina bar with a level badge, top-right circular minimap, a 6-slot item hotbar at the bottom center, a small quest banner reading short text. Polished mobile/console casual game look, crisp, inviting, NOT photoreal, NOT dark.`

## Theme B — Dark Nordic Epic

`A third-person fantasy action-RPG game screenshot, blocky low-poly world with a moody Nordic Skyrim mood. A low-poly hero with a sword stands on a stone path on a snowy windswept mountain at dusk, facing a ruined stone keep with a glowing orange torch-lit dungeon entrance ahead; a large dragon silhouette circles a distant jagged peak through cold fog. Desaturated cool palette, dramatic rim lighting, overcast moody sky, drifting snow and mist, atmospheric depth, torch-glow accents. Readable but restrained game UI overlay: top-left health and stamina bars with a level badge, top-right circular minimap, a 6-slot hotbar at the bottom center, a small quest banner. Stylized low-poly, cinematic, mature but still bright enough to read, NOT photoreal, NOT gory.`

## Theme C — Stylized Painterly Low-poly

`A third-person fantasy action-RPG game screenshot, premium hand-painted stylized low-poly world (Genshin / Oceanhorn quality bar). A low-poly hero with a sword stands on a stone path on a snowy mountain in warm golden-hour light, facing a ruined stone keep with a glowing dungeon entrance ahead; a graceful dragon silhouette circles a distant peak. Rich warm-to-cool gradients, painterly soft textures, gentle bloom, lush polished art direction, strong silhouette readability. Clean modern game UI overlay: top-left health and stamina bars with a level badge, top-right circular minimap, a 6-slot hotbar at the bottom center, a small quest banner. Beautiful premium stylized mobile-RPG look, NOT photoreal, NOT flat.`

## Notes

- All 3 must keep the SAME framing, HUD layout, and key landmarks (hero, path,
  keep entrance, dragon, peak) so the comparison isolates art theme.
- Output: PNG saved to disk; intake copies the accepted one(s) into the project
  visual section. Reject baked-in fake letters in the HUD if illegible — UI text
  is placeholder for direction only.
