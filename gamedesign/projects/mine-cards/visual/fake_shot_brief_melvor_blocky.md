# Fake Shot Brief - Block Mining Idle v0.01

## Visual Session Contract

- Goal: produce a first visual target for a Melvor-like block mining idle RPG.
- Non-goal: no runtime implementation, no card combat, no final asset pack, no
  copied Minecraft/Melvor UI.
- Proof: one portrait fake gameplay screenshot plus a written review packet.
- Stop condition: if the fake shot reads as card battler, poster, or full
  multi-skill clone, regenerate before implementation.
- Likely files: this brief, generated fake shot, updated reference intake,
  later runtime screenshot mismatch list.

## Recommended Fake Shot

Fake shot tier: `gameplay fake shot`.

Purpose: test whether `Mining-only idle v0.01 + blocky mine style` is the right
first-screen direction.

## Must Show

- Portrait mobile composition, `1080x1920` feel.
- Original blocky voxel miner hero, not Steve/Minecraft.
- Active skill panel: `Mining`.
- Selected node: `Copper Vein` or `Surface Stone`.
- Large progress bar and timer.
- Resource feedback: ore, coins, Mining XP, mastery.
- Clear next goal: upgrade to `Copper Pickaxe`.
- One locked/deeper node preview.
- Bottom or side nav can be minimal; avoid a giant full-product tab bar.
- Background: cozy voxel mine/workshop, but UI remains primary.

## Must Avoid

- No cards.
- No combat.
- No spider mine goal in v0.01 fake shot.
- Do not copy Minecraft blocks, Steve, creepers, logos, or item silhouettes.
- Do not copy Melvor UI layout verbatim.
- Do not bake tiny unreadable text as final.
- Do not make it a poster; it must look like a playable game screen.
- Do not show 20 skills on first screen.
