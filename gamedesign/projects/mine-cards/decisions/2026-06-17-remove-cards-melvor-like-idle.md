# Decision: Remove Cards From v0.01 And Pivot To Melvor-Like Idle

Date: 2026-06-17

Status: accepted direction for next design iteration.

## Decision

`Mine Cards` keeps its working name for now, but cards are removed from the
first playable direction.

The new first direction is:

```text
Melvor-like block mining idle RPG in the user's original mine universe.
```

## Why

The current reference preference is closer to Melvor Idle than the old card
descent design. Melvor's own early changelog suggests the safe way to approach a
large idle RPG is to start with one skill and expand only after the first loop is
clear.

## Consequences

- v0.01 is Mining-only.
- Old battle/card PSDs are historical reference, not first-slice requirement.
- Combat, equipment, smithing, and card expeditions are deferred until Mining
  proves the loop.
- Fake shots should show a Mining activity screen, not cards or combat.
- Runtime work remains blocked until fake-shot direction and first-slice
  numbers are accepted.
