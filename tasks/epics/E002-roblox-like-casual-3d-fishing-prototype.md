## Goal

Design and build an original Roblox-like casual 3D fishing prototype for native
PC first: bright juicy visuals, understandable fishing gameplay, generated UI
and art pipeline proof, and passive profiling of the AI workflow.

## In scope

- Project wiki under `gamedesign/projects/roblox-fishing/`.
- Reference research for Roblox-like fishing, cozy/social fishing, progression,
  and asset options.
- Lead review before locking the concept, fake shot, and GDD.
- Generated art/UI direction with reusable runtime-asset thinking.
- Native PC playable 3D prototype with screenshot/input proof.
- Passive profiling notes for skill/tool friction and useful workflow lessons.

## Out of scope

- Editing `external/neotolis-engine` unless explicitly requested.
- Web/mobile/browser prototype work unless the lead explicitly approves it.
- Copying protected names, screenshots, UI layouts, or Roblox/Fisch assets.
- Final monetization systems, live-service code, or multiplayer networking in
  the first prototype.
