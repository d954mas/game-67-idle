## Goal

Build an original casual open-world fantasy RPG for native PC and web/mobile,
inspired by the player fantasy of Skyrim, Daggerfall-scale breadth, and
The Quest-style readable RPG interaction, then prepare it for Poki-audience
testing.

## In scope

- Original fantasy setting and GDD in `gamedesign/projects/rune-marches/`.
- Native-first playable slice with magic, progression, map unlock, main quest,
  side quest seed, and recoverable combat.
- Web/mobile portrait build after native proof because the user explicitly
  requested web/mobile.
- FTUE, visual readability, balance, and test packet tasks.

## Out of scope

- Editing `external/neotolis-engine`.
- Copying Elder Scrolls or The Quest IP, names, exact UI, quests, or lore.
- Full open-world production before first slice proof.
- Poki submission claims before a validated web build and test plan.
